    Who we are and what we do

    Who we are?

    Joshua Allen owns HIE and writes, funds and produces feature motion pictures for distribution on digital platforms. As all projects are the sole proprietary and intellectual property of HIE, we support the 'share' community and encourage file sharing of our works.

    Discover More

    What we do?

    HIE is an experimental filmmaking venture that writes original works and via the 'Live-Work' (Out-of-Pocket) model produces motion pictures. The works are then distributed on the various 'On-Demand' pay platforms and screened at film festivals all over the world.

    Our literary works are available online or per request. As most of our screenplays are not available to the public due to intellectual property concerns we ask that you contact us with a letter of intent. Works may be optioned or purchased.
